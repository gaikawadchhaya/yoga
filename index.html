Yoga is a holistic practice that integrates physical postures, breath control, meditation,
and ethical principles to promote physical health, mental clarity, and spiritual growth.

  1. 1. Physical Postures (Asanas)

    2. Yoga includes a variety of physical postures known as asanas, which are designed to improve
